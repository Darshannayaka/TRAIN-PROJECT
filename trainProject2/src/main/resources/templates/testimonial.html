Board and Managing Director

Smt. Rajni Hasija

Chairman & Managing Director

Smt. Rajni Hasija is the Whole - Time Director (Tourism & Marketing) of our Company. She has been on the Board of our Company since May 18, 2018. She is an officer of Indian Railway Traffic Service (IRTS) of 1989 batch.

Shri Ajit Kumar

Director (Finance)

Shri Ajit Kumar, Indian Railway Account Service (IRAS) of 1989 Batch has vast experience in different organizations of Railways as well as outside bodies. he has worked in Diesel Locomotives.
